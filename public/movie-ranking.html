The Best Movies According to Jia

My top 3 movies of all time.

Titanic

This is my most favorite film. Directed by James Cameron. Powerhouse cast led by Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet.

The Devil Wears Prada

A movie about fashion. Said to be semi-fictional. Casts include Meryl Streep, Anne Hathaway, and Emily Blunt.

A Boy Called Christmas

A fantasy, Christmas-themed film starring Dame Maggie Smith.
